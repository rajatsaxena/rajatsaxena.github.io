About

I am currently a 2nd year PhD student in the Neurobiology and Behavior department at UC Irvine, advised by Dr Bruce L McNaughton. My research focuses on figuring out how short term memories are consolidated into long term memories through hippocampal-cortical interaction. Previously, I worked as Research Assistant at IISc, Bangalore in Dr Sachin Deshmukh's lab where I worked on understanding the neural correlates of spatial navigation and memory and also developed camera systems to track rodents in large spaces.


Intrigued by almost everything related to engineering, mathematics, philosophy, design and psychology. I like to hack around building machine vision and behavior tracking systems. Most of my non-coding time is spent reading, playing football, rock climbing or watching tv shows.
